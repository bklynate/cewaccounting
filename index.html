Accounting and Tax Services

Full serivce accounting services that cater to all needs.

Tax Prep

Preperation of Individual, S-Corporation, C-Corporation, and Partnership Tax Returns which include Federal and all State level filings.

Bookkeeping

In-depth and organized bookkeeping services that will keep your financial records up to date and presented on detailed Income Statement and Balance Sheet in order to effectively prepare your various Tax Returns.

Payroll Reporting

Preparation of quarterly Federal and State payroll tax returns and year-end payroll returns such as Form 940 and W-3 and W-2. In addition, this includes the preparation of various informational returns such as Form 1099, 1099-R, 1099-DIV, and 1099-INT.
